About This Calculator

The Streaming Royalties Calculator is a simple tool that helps artists, producers, and independent creators estimate their potential earnings from music streaming platforms. By entering your stream numbers, you can instantly see how much you might earn across services like Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, and more.

The calculator uses average industry payout rates to give you a realistic approximation of your royalties. It's designed to be easy to use, transparent, and helpful for understanding your streaming income, comparing platforms, and getting a clearer picture of how your music performs financially.

Important Note: All calculations are estimates based on average industry payout rates and may vary depending on factors like territory, subscription type, and platform agreements.

Who Is This For?

This calculator is perfect for:

  • Independent Artists - Track your streaming income across platforms
  • Producers & Songwriters - Estimate royalty earnings from collaborations
  • Music Managers - Compare platform performance for your artists
  • Labels & Distributors - Forecast revenue from streaming releases

How It Works

Simply enter the number of streams for each platform, adjust your royalty share percentage (if you split earnings with collaborators), and select your preferred currency. The calculator will instantly show you:

  • Gross total earnings across all platforms
  • Your share after royalty splits
  • Platform-by-platform breakdown of earnings
